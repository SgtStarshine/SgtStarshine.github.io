Off the Hook Fan Site

Bio

Off the Hook is a pop idol duo consisting of only two members, inkling rapper Pearl and octoling DJ Marina.

The two members first met in a place called Mt. Nantai, where they formed a band after sharing their respective musical talents. Marina, who hailed from the Octo Canyon, was taught Inkling culture by Pearl and the two have been close ever since, although for a while Pearl was not aware of Marina's history as an Octarian soldier.
